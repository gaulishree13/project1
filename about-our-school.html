About our school

Situated in the heart of the Pokhara Valley, Kalika Model Secondary School is a non-profitable public academic institution recognized by the Government of Nepal, Ministry of Education since 2021 B.S. and HSEB in .2057 B.S. for running classes from nursery to +2 level. Kalika Public College, affiliated to T.U., has been running under the same roof. Kalika Model Secondary School and Kalika Public College have a virtually integrated and inseparable existence. English medium classes are running up to grade X. Upgraded every new year. In +2 level, classes are being run in Science, Management, Education and Humanity subjects in both Nepali and English medium. The Government of Nepal has declared this school as a model school since 2074. Nowadays it's educational, physical and administrative improvement works are being done for model school. Kalika Public College offers classes in the Education and Management in bachelor's degree. Education is the most basic component to lead a man towards the right path of life. Kalika Model Secondary School ensures practical education in affordable fee to the grass root level students in a fully facilitating and friendly environment. The school is easily accessible from any part of the valley as well as neighbouring villages. It has become the only academic destination for those students who seek for quality and practical education in reasonable monthly fees.
